# About

This repository contains a comprehensive academic report on Media and Communication, structured into units covering various aspects of the field.

## Overview

The report explores the foundations, types, systems, ethics, journalism, digital media, and public relations in contemporary media and communication.

## Structure

- **[Introduction](/introduction/)** - Main report introduction and course overview
- **[Unit 1: Principles of Mass Communication](/unit1/)** - Foundations of Communication
- **[Unit 2: Media and Society](/unit2/)** - Types of Communication
- **[Unit 3: Introduction to Communication Studies](/unit3/)** - Media Systems and Institutions
- **[Unit 4: History of Mass Communication](/unit4/)** - Media Ethics and Law
- **[Unit 5: English for Mass Communication](/unit5/)** - Journalism and Reporting
- **[Unit 6: Critical Thinking and Problem Solving](/unit6/)** - Digital and Social Media
- **[Unit 7: Communication and Collaboration Skills](/unit7/)** - Public Relations and Advertising

## Getting Started

1. Start with the [Introduction](/introduction/) to understand the scope and objectives
2. Progress through each unit sequentially
3. Refer to additional resources in the footer
