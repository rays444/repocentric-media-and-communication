## Author Introduction

MR OJO is a student of Media and Communication Studies with a strong interest in leadership, research, and innovation. He is the second-born in a family of four, an experience that has shaped his sense of responsibility, cooperation, and commitment to growth.

He is an avid reader who values knowledge as a tool for personal development and societal change. His passion for leadership is driven by purpose rather than position, and he believes in leading by example. Over time, he has actively engaged in leadership roles, developing skills in organization, communication, and service to others.

In addition to leadership and communication, he has a keen interest in research and programming, reflecting his curiosity and problem-solving mindset. He enjoys exploring new ideas, analyzing information, and applying technology to create practical solutions.

His hobbies include listening to music, with a particular appreciation for love music and diverse musical genres, which he finds both inspiring and relaxing. He is also deeply committed to giving back to the community and enjoys helping people freely, guided by the belief that true leadership is rooted in service.

Driven by passion and experience, OJO aspires to continue serving in leadership positions and welcomes opportunities, including elections, where his dedication, experience, and people-centered approach can contribute meaningfully to collective progress.
