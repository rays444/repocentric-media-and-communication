# Media & Communication Academic Roadmap

Welcome to the comprehensive Media and Communication academic resource. This site contains structured learning materials across seven units covering all aspects of contemporary media and communication studies.

## 📚 Course Units

### [Unit 1: Principles of Mass Communication](/01_Unit_One_Principles_of_Mass_Communication/)

Foundations of Communication theory and core concepts.

### [Unit 2: Media and Society](/02_Unit_Two_Media_and_Society/)

Understanding different types of communication and their societal impact.

### [Unit 3: Introduction to Communication Studies](/03_Unit_Three_Introduction_to_Communication_Studies/)

Media systems and institutional structures.

### [Unit 4: History of Mass Communication](/04_Unit_Four_History_of_Mass_Communication/)

Media ethics and legal frameworks.

### [Unit 5: English for Mass Communication](/05_Unit_Five_English_for_Mass_Communication/)

Journalism and professional reporting techniques.

### [Unit 6: Critical Thinking and Problem Solving](/06_Unit_Six_Critical_Thinking_and_Problem_Solving/)

Digital media trends and social media analysis.

### [Unit 7: Communication and Collaboration Skills](/07_Unit_Seven_Communication_and_Collaboration_Skills/)

Public Relations and advertising strategies.
